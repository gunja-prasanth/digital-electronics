# Practice Problems — Registers

## Basic

Design 4-bit register.

Answer:
Use 4 D flip-flops connected to same clock.

---

## Medium

What happens when EN = 0?

Answer:
Register holds previous value.

---

## Hard

Design 4-bit shift register.

Answer:

* Connect output of one flip-flop to next input
* Data shifts every clock pulse

---

## Interview Question

Difference between register and flip-flop?

Answer:

* Flip-flop → stores 1 bit
* Register → stores multiple bits
